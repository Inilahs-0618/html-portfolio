My top 3 series recommendations

The Vampire Diaries

A romantic thriller, this series is a love triangle between Elena and the Salvatore brothers, Damon and Stefan

Squid Game

Trapped in a mysterious island, people struggle to survive by playing deadly games. Their only way out? They have to get through all games

All of us are dead

Outbreak of an unknown virus in Hyosan High forces students to take refuge in the school itself. Will they ever escape from the deadly zombies?
